INSTRUCTIONS

Concentration, is also known as Memory Match, Pelmanism,Shinkei-suija, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards
